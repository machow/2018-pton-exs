String Methods and Lists

names = "amy greg todd" # fill in the blank split_names = ___ # add jane and pablo to names names = "amy greg todd" # fill in the blank split_names = names.split(" ") split_names.append("jane") split_names.append("pablo") Ex().check_object('split_names').is_instance(list) Ex().has_equal_value("By the end, does `split_names` have 5 entries?", expr_code = "len('split_names')") Ex().test_object('split_names')

Instructions

  • split the variable names by its spaces. Assign the result to split_names
  • add the names "jane" and "pablo" to the end of split_names
